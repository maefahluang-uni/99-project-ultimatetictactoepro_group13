Game Instructions

The game is built on a 3x3 grid, but each cell contains another smaller 3x3 grid.

How to play?

- Player X starts by marking the first move on any empty cell in one of the smaller tic-tac-toe boards.

- Player O then clicks on an empty cell in a smaller board corresponding to the opponent's last move.

- After the initial moves, players take turns to make the moves, but only on the small board corresponding to the opponent's last move.

Winning Condition

- When a player wins a smaller board, they will get the cell in the larger 3X3 grid.

- The game is won when the player successfully wins three smaller boards in a row (horizontally, vertically, diagonally) OR when a player wins more smaller boards than another player.
